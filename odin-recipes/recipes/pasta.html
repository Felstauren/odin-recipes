Ingredients

  • Regular or whole wheat spaghetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh or dried chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook your spaghetti until al dente
  2. Meantime, fry gently garlic and chili in olive oil
  3. Drain spaghetti, reserve 1/2 cup cooking water
  4. Add spaghetti and parley to the skillet
  5. Add as much cooking water as needed to make the pasta moist
